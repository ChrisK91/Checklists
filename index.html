Welcome

Here I collect a variety of medical checklists. They come from a variety of sources, including articles, books, and personal experience. For obvious reasons, you should not rely on these lists alone, as I cannot guarantee that the will always be correct. This page is very much a work in progress. If you are interested in the source code used to generate these files, you can find them via the button below.

The name of the sites comes from medical checklist.

Use the “Checklists”-Button in the top right to get started.
